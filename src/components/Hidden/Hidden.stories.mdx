# Hidden

The Hidden component will unmount children components given they meet the breakpoint criteria of the `isSmall`, `isMedium`, `isLarge` and/or `isXLarge` props.

This example has a `isSmall`, `isMedium` prop of true, and therefore will not be mounted at those breakpoints.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Base component">
    <Hidden
      isLarge={knobs.boolean('isLarge', false, 'Presentation')}
      isMedium={knobs.boolean('isMedium', true, 'Presentation')}
      isSmall={knobs.boolean('isSmall', true, 'Presentation')}
      isXLarge={knobs.boolean('isXLarge', false, 'Presentation')}
    >
      <DefinitionList items={DefinitionListFixture.items} />
    </Hidden>
  </Story>
</Preview>
